# Quick Reference Card

## Event Classification

| Type | Question | Examples |
|------|----------|----------|
| 🔴 Industry Shock | What capacity changed? | Refinery fire, demand collapse |
| 🟡 Policy | What changed legally? | Sanctions, tax policy |
| 🔵 Sentiment | What do people expect? | Analyst forecasts, polls |

## Scoring Guide

| Score | Meaning | Position |
|-------|---------|----------|
| 0-20 | Severe victim | Short |
| 21-40 | Clear victim | Underweight |
| 41-60 | Neutral | Market weight |
| 61-80 | Clear beneficiary | Overweight |
| 81-100 | Major beneficiary | Long |

## Signal Thresholds

| Type | Strong | Moderate | Weak |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| Score divergence | ≥5% | ≥3% | ≥1.5% |
| Time lag | >4 hrs | >1 hr | >15 min |
| Cross-market | >5% | >3% | >1% |
